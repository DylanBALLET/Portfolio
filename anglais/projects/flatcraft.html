Flatcraft

Flatcraft is a collaborative project by a team of 4 as part of the IUT. This project had primarily an educational purpose, serving to develop our skills in Java. Flatcraft is simply a 2D game similar to Minecraft with blocks and crafting!

The game is composed of several biomes:

  • Overworld (the basic biome)
  • The Nether (the underworld)
  • And the End (end game biome)

So in Flatcraft, we can explore the world, change biomes, craft tools and weapons, refine materials, and cook food in the furnace.
