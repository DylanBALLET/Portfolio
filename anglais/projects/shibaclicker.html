ShibaClicker

ShibaClicker is a personal project, more of a game than a website. It is a "clicker" type game where the goal is to acquire the most power by clicking on a Shiba and buying weapons for more efficiency!

When clicking on the dog, we gain +1 power, and it's possible to improve click power by buying upgrades.

But the game is not just about clicking! You need to buy weapons to gain power passively (1 shuriken = 0.1 power per second, etc.).

The game is still in development, but basic functions are already implemented, such as multiple purchases.
